Program sft2warp | Time-squared warping. 
[SYNOPSIS]
sft2warp < in.rsf > out.rsf inv=n adj=n pad=n1 eps=0.01
[PARAMETERS]
bool    | adj | =n |  [y/n] | 	adjoint flag 
float   | eps | =0.01 |   | 	stretch regularization 
bool    | inv | =n |  [y/n] | 	inversion flag 
int     | pad | =n1 |   | 	output time samples

system/generic
