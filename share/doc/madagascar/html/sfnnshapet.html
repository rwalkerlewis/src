Synopsis
        sfnnshapet < in.rsf > out.rsf coord=coord.rsf pattern=pattern.rsf weight=weight.rsf n1= n2= d1=1. d2=1. o1=0. o2=0. niter=10 niter=niter eps=1.0e-6 rect1=1 rect2=1 rect3=1 nw=2 sym=n tol=1e-3
