sfvti3dpseudosv (2.1-git)		index
user/chengjb/Mvti3dpseudosv.c
3-D three-components wavefield modeling using pseudo-pure mode SV-wave equation in 3D VTI media.

 
Synopsis
        sfvti3dpseudosv < Fvp0.rsf vs0=Fvs0.rsf epsi=Fep.rsf del=Fde.rsf > Fo1.rsf PseudoPureSVz=Fo2.rsf PseudoPureSV=Fo3.rsf ns=301 dt=0.001 bd=20
* Note: The x- and y-components of pseudo-pure-mode qSV-wave are summed thus the
* 2nd-order system only consist of two equations.
