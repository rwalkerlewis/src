sfort3dhomodevcK (2.1-git)		index
user/chengjb/Mort3dhomodevcK.c
Correct projection deviation in K-domian for 3-D pseudo-pure P-wave field in homogeneous ORT media.

 
Synopsis
        sfort3dhomodevcK < Fix.rsf apvy=Fiy.rsf apvz=Fiz.rsf PseudoPurePx=Fi1.rsf PseudoPurePy=Fi2.rsf PseudoPurePz=Fi3.rsf > Fo1.rsf PseudoPureSepP=Fo2.rsf
