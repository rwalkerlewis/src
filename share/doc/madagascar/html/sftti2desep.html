sftti2desep (2.1-git)		index
user/chengjb/Mtti2desep.c
2-D two-components wavefield modeling using original elastic displacement wave equation in TTI media.

 
Synopsis
        sftti2desep < Fvp0.rsf vs0=Fvs0.rsf epsi=Feps.rsf del=Fdel.rsf the=Fthe.rsf > Fo1.rsf Elasticz=Fo2.rsf apx=Fo3.rsf apz=Fo4.rsf apxs=Fo5.rsf apzs=Fo6.rsf apxx=Fo7.rsf apzz=Fo8.rsf apxxs=Fo9.rsf apzzs=Fo10.rsf ElasticSepP=Fo11.rsf ElasticSepSV=Fo12.rsf ns=301 dt=0.001 isep=0 ihomo=0 nstep=1 tapertype=
